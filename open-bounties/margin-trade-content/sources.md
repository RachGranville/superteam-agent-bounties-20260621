Relevant source facts from the listing/docs:

- Margin Trade is non-custodial and onchain.
- It uses a central limit order book and settles onchain.
- It supports crypto, equities, commodities, and more.
- It currently uses cross margin.
- PnL and liquidations use mark price.
- Distressed positions move through liquidation, backstop, and ADL stages.
