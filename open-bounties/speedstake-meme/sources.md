Relevant source facts:

- Speedstake lets Solana stakers receive SOL today by selling future staking rewards.
- The principal remains staked/delegated.
- The staker sells the future rewards stream, not the principal.
- Pye describes the flow as non-custodial.
- Pye charges a 0.50% protocol fee on SOL proceeds at sale according to the Speedstake page.
